The Goldman Sachs Group, Inc.

The Goldman Sachs Group, Inc. is an American multinational investment bank and financial services company. Founded in 1869, Goldman Sachs is headquartered in Lower Manhattan in New York City, with regional headquarters in many international financial centers.

Key Global Standing

  • Goldman Sachs is one of the largest investment banks in the world by revenue.
  • Ranked 55th on the Fortune 500 list of the largest United States corporations by total revenue.
  • In the Forbes Global 2000 of 2024, Goldman Sachs ranked 23rd.
  • It is considered a systemically important financial institution (SIFI) by the Financial Stability Board.

Core Service Offerings

Goldman Sachs offers a comprehensive suite of financial services, including:

  • Investment Banking: Advisory for mergers and acquisitions and restructuring, and securities underwriting.
  • Global Markets: Prime brokerage, market making for financial products, and flow trading (on behalf of clients) and proprietary trading (for its own account).
  • Asset & Wealth Management: Asset management and wealth management services.
  • Other Services: Clearing and custodian bank services, operation of private-equity funds and hedge funds, structuring of complex and tailor-made financial products.

The company invests in and arranges financing for startups, often getting additional business as a bookrunner when those companies launch initial public offerings (IPOs).
